About Us

Welcome to our Automated Lung Cancer Detection System! Our goal is to help detect lung cancer early,when it is most treatable.
We believe that by increasing awareness about lung cancer and providing an easy-to-use tool for detecting the disease, we can
help save lives.Lung cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the lungs. It usually develops in the cells that line the air passages.
Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer death in the United States and around the world. Smoking is the primary cause of
lung cancer, but other factors can also increase the risk of developing the disease.

How It Works

Our website uses a CT Image of your lung, symptoms, and generate a results. The result can help you determine whether you should seek
further medical attention.We take accuracy and reliability very seriously. Our results has been extensively validated using large datasets,
and its ability to accurately assess your risk for lung cancer is the best.

Privacy and Security

Our Mission

Our mission is to increase awareness about lung cancer and provide an easy-to-use tool for detecting the disease early. We believe that
early detection is key to improving survival rates and reducing the impact of lung cancer on individuals and families.We encourage you to
use our website to assess your risk for lung cancer and take action to prevent the disease. Remember that early detection is key to improving
survival rates, so don't hesitate to talk to your doctor if you have any concerns about your lung health.

Our Team

Our team is made up of doctors, researchers, and developers who are passionate about improving lung cancer detection. We have worked
together to develop a reliable and accurate risk assessment tool for lung cancer.
